St Aidan's College SCR

Branding, Web Design, Information Architecture, Book Design

The Senior Common Room at St Aidan's College, Durham Univeristy needed a new website to share service and event information with their members. The previous website had not been maintained for a number of years, which had impacted on the membership and event attendance numbers.

The brief for this project was to perform organise the vast amount of information in an accessible way, while also updating the look and feel of the site.

The College crest was incorporated into a sophisticated branding for the website as seen in the screenshots above. In addition to the online information, the SCR also wanted to disemintate welcome information to new students through a printed handbook. This was the second stage of the project, and required a more 'modern' branding to reflect the student life at the St Aidan's. The resultant book design has been used for each new intake of students since its completion.
