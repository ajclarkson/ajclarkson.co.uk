PyMap

Python Development, IMAP Integration

PyMap is a basic Python IMAP Email processing program. At the moment it checks your 'Pending' emails folder for outstanding mail and emails you a friendly reminder that some of them need your attention. It is very early stages of development at the moment, and I expect to add more functionality as the application evolves.

  • Taking Control of Email

    When you have a lot of email to stay on top of it's all too easy to get swamped, and one or two inevitably fall through the cracks. Now that can either be inconvienent or distastorous. There's hundreds of ways of staying on top of email out there, but I decided to build one that solves a specific problem.
