Michelle Ormrod Pilates

Branding, Web Design, Backend Development, Stationary Design

Michelle Ormrod wanted a new website to advertise her Pilates business in Darlington and the North East. The brief was to create a site which informed her clients on class locations and times, and was recently expanded to reflect her new venture as the owner of the Pilates & Health Studio in Darlington.

The site runs on the PerchCMS and, with the addition of bespoke functionality, allows for a completely flexible timetable which expands and contracts depending on the number of columns added. Some more custom functionality also provides Michelle with an easy way to link a timetable entry to a practicioner on the studio details page, all without her having to write a single line of code.

As part of the original brief Michelle's branding was given a modern update, and a range of stationary was designed to meet her business needs.
