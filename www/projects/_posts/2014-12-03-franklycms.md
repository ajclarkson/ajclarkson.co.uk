It's a lightweight CMS based on the Ruby Sinatra framework (see what I did there?), aimed at taking a collection of articles in a git repository, applying a layout template and generating site navigation for them. It's development is on hold at the minute as I found and started using Jekyll for this site, but I do plan to return to it in future and continue to make it the ultimate minimal writing CMS.
