<p>PyMap is a basic Python IMAP Email processing program. At the moment it checks your 'Pending' emails folder for outstanding mail and emails you a friendly reminder that some of them need your attention. It is very early stages of development at the moment, and I expect to add more functionality as the application evolves.</p>
</section>
