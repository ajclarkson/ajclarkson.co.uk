<p>Tom Brock neeed a website to showcase his publication record and speaking events, as well as providing social media links to contact him. The content was key here, so clear balanced typography provided maximum readability. To inject some of his personality into the site, he asked me to create a pixel art representation of him as a profile picture.</p>

<p>The site is fully responsive and adapts to whatever screen size the website is viewed on, collapsing columns and re-structuring lists as neccessary.</p>
