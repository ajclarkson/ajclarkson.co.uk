<section class="project-info">
<p>The Senior Common Room at St Aidan's College, Durham Univeristy needed a new website to share service and event information with their members. The previous website had not been maintained for a number of years, which had impacted on the membership and event attendance numbers.</p>

<p>The brief for this project was to perform organise the vast amount of information in an accessible way, while also updating the look and feel of the site.</p>

</section>

<section class="project-info">
<p>The College crest was incorporated into a sophisticated branding for the website as seen in the screenshots above. In addition to the online information, the SCR also wanted to disemintate welcome information to new students through a printed handbook. This was the second stage of the project, and required a more 'modern' branding to reflect the student life at the St Aidan's. The resultant book design has been used for each new intake of students since its completion.</p>
