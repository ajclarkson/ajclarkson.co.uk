<section class="project-info">
<p>Michelle Ormrod wanted a new website to advertise her Pilates business in Darlington and the North East. The brief was to create a site which informed her clients on class locations and times, and was recently expanded to reflect her new venture as the owner of the Pilates &amp; Health Studio in Darlington.</p>
<p>The site runs on the PerchCMS and, with the addition of bespoke functionality, allows for a completely flexible timetable which expands and contracts depending on the number of columns added. Some more custom functionality also provides Michelle with an easy way to link a timetable entry to a practicioner on the studio details page, all without her having to write a single line of code.</p>
