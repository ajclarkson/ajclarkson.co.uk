    • About Me

    I'm Adam, a designer & developer who is currently writing a PhD on Content Delivery in Augmented Reality Systems.

    My PhD takes up a huge portion of my time at the minute. Time that is spent delving through Computer Vision problems, and looking at new ways we can deliver content into often unpredicatble and unstable Augmented Reality environments.

    When I'm not working on that I'm usually pushing pixels around the web, smudging pencil sketches, or worrying about gaining an extra millisecond of efficiency out of some code. When I do manage to break free from my computer, I get out cycling or disappear into my kitchen and brew up some craft beer.

    Social Lurkings

    I can also be found in the usual dark corners of the internet, where if you're interested you can take a look at my opinions, photos, designs, code, and laziness cycling activity.

    About This Site

    I've started fresh with this version of the site, cast aside old blog posts and such, and moved to a new system in Jekyll. It gives me the flexibility to customise every aspect of each post if I like. As such the templates that you see now have been lovingly hand coded by me before being converted to a set of static pages by Jekyll. That provides you with a fast and reliable experience when browsing this site.

    The type on this site is set in FF Meta Serif, with the sans-serif variant in use for sub-headings and meta data. Code examples are set in Source Code Pro, all fonts are served by Typekit.
