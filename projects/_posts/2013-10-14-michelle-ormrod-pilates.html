Identity Update, Website Design & Stationary

{{page.excerpt}}

Relaxation to the Core

Michelle had a very strong desire to update her old branding and website to a more calm colour scheme and modern feel. A minimal template for the Perch CMS and a tweak to the typography and colour scheme of the branding achieved this.

Content Focus

The site is here to provide class information for prospective and current clients. To that end, the design gets out of the way when it comes to presenting the key information.

Business Stationary

To fall in line with the branding refresh, a new set of stationary including letterheads and business card were designed. These allow Michelle to keep a consistent brand across all communication with her clients.
