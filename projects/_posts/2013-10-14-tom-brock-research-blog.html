Wordpress Theme Design, Plugin & Function Customisation

{{page.excerpt}}

Clear Content Focus

The value of this site is in it's content. The blog posts are often long form, and as such it was imperitive to make sure that it was comfortable to read. By paying close attention to the typography, and removing any distracting sidebars, the reader is directed straight to the content they need to see.

Promoting Social Media

Tom is very active on both twitter and readability, so it made sense to promote that fact on his site. Following the content first ethos of the site, these were moved to an expanded footer at the bottom of his pages. That way they don't distract from the content, but are still easily accessible.

Augmenting Wordpress Functions

Additional functions were added to the wordpress install to provide administrator access to edit every aspect of the site, and to provide a number of shortcodes for custom placement of widgets.
