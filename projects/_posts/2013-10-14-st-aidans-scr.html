Identity, Website Design, & Handbook Layout

{{page.excerpt}}

Clear, Recognisable Branding

The College has a well established brand within Durham Unviersity, and so the Senior Common Room site had to covey this. By focusing on the College crest, and chosing the green College colour, the site fell in line with other media elements.

Information Architecture

The SCR has to provide a wealth of information to current and prospective students. However, the site shouldn't appear overwhelming, which meant keeping the top level navigation simple. Utilising some jQuery to show and hide content allowed more commonly searched information to take the centre stage.

Consolidating the Brand

The College sends a handbook to all new postgraduate students before they start. The handbook information was due to be updated at the same time as this web project, and so a new layout was commissioned to fall in line with the new website look and feel.
