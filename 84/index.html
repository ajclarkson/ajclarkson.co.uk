General Information

For sale an attractive three bedroom semi-detached house. Situated on a popular estate of similar properties that is within reasonable distance of Castleford town centre, and has convenient access to Pontefract, the A1 and M62.

Briefly comprising of entrance hall, lounge, kitchen diner with three bedrooms and family bathroom to first floor. Externally there are front and rear gardens with driveway and garage. This property has cavity wall insulation throughout and 250mm fully insulated loft An internal viewing of this property is highly recommended.
